Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Welcome to Today's Fable. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure, tranquil reading experience. We collect the following types of information:

  • Profile Information: When you enter your name or log in, we store this locally on your device to personalize your journey.
  • Reading Progress: We track your reading streaks, stories read, and unlocked badges locally to gamify your wisdom journey.
  • App Preferences: Your choices regarding immersive audio and daily reminders are stored locally to remember your setup.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly used to enhance your personal in-app experience:

  • To display your name and personalize story completion messages.
  • To maintain your Wisdom XP, day streaks, and profile level.
  • To remember hardware preferences (like sound enablement) to reduce friction on return visits.

3. Data Storage & Security

Unless connected to a cloud sync service (such as Google Authentication), all your personal data remains entirely local to your device using browser LocalStorage. We do not sell, rent, or secretly transmit your reading habits to third-party ad networks.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use secure third-party services exclusively for providing essential application infrastructure, content delivery, and optional secure cloud authentication. These providers are bound by their own stringent privacy standards.

5. Your Rights & Control

Because most data is stored locally, you have complete control. You can clear your app data at any time by clearing your browser site data or using the "Sign Out" function, which resets your local profile.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
